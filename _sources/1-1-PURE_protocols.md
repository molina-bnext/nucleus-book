# PURE Protocols

## Getting Started

PURE (”**P**rotein Synthesis **U**sing **R**ecombinant **E**lements”) is a defined cell-free system capable of transcribing mRNAs (using the T7 RNA polymerase) and translating proteins (using *E. coli* ribosomes and translation factors). PURE is built from individually purified components — proteins, RNAs, organic molecules, etc. The protocols here cover expressing and purifying protein components, preparing energy solutions and buffers, and assembling a complete PURE reaction.
